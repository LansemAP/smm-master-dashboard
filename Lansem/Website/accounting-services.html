Service Scope

UK-Specific Accounting Services Scope

Understand exactly what tasks our specialists handle and where the line of regulatory responsibility is drawn.

Accounting Service Included? Operational Scope & Details
Bank Feed Reconciliation Yes Daily matching of bank feed transactions in Xero, QuickBooks Online, or other ledger software.
Receipt Coding & Matching Yes Processing, coding, and receipt matching via automation tools like Dext or Hubdoc.
Payroll RTI Calculation Yes Processing payroll runs, RTI, FPS, EPS calculations, and drafting auto-enrolment pension sheets (BrightPay).
VAT Return Preparation Yes MTD-compliant transactional compilation, bank-to-ledger checks, and draft preparation.
Management Accounts Drafts Yes Monthly/quarterly drafting of Profit & Loss (P&L), Balance Sheet, and ledger adjustments.
FRS 102 / 105 Accounts Drafts Yes Advanced prep sheets, working papers, and draft accounts prepared by CA/ACCA-level specialists.
CIS Scheme Returns Yes Compilation and calculation of Construction Industry Scheme deductions and subcontractor statements.
Company Formation & Tax Advice No General tax consulting, corporate structure advice, and UK company formation (requires separate authorization).
HMRC Tax Submissions No Direct electronic filing or submission of VAT, CIS, or Corporate Tax returns to HMRC.
Audit Work & Sign-off No Registered statutory audit procedures or audit opinions (requires UK registered auditor status).
⚠️ Important Regulatory Boundary

LANSEM operates as a white-label back-office processor. We compile, reconcile, and draft reports for your client ledgers, but we never submit tax returns to HMRC or sign off audits. The final review, adjustments, and electronic submission responsibilities remain strictly with your UK firm's licensed practitioners.
