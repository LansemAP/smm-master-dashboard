Who We Are

Grow Your Career with LANSEM

At LANSEM, we deliver premium legal and accounting support services exclusively for UK-based professional practices. We build elite teams of Chartered Accountants, ACCA certified professionals, and legal advisors who work on complex, high-value client portfolios.

Why join us? We operate a remote-first, high-performance workspace where continuous learning is mandatory. You will be trained in international cloud systems, UK GAAP (FRS 102/105), VAT regulations, and global client reporting standards, giving you rare exposure and valuable expertise.

If you are looking for structured career progression, competitive global compensation, and a team that values your technical capabilities, explore our open tiers below.

Structured Transition

Our 8-Week Transition Program

We transition Indian CA finalists and professionals into UK-ready client delivery specialists through a dedicated onboarding bootcamp.

Weeks 1–2

UK Cloud Software Ecosystem

Master the user interfaces, bank feed matching rules, and automation systems used by UK small businesses.

  • Complete Xero Advisor Certification (Xero Central)
  • Complete QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor Certification
  • Practical training on Dext / Hubdoc and OCR categorization rules
Weeks 3–4

UK VAT & CIS Regulations

Understand standard UK transactional taxes, reverse charge mechanisms, and construction industry withholdings.

  • VAT Schemes: Standard, Flat Rate, and Cash Accounting
  • Making Tax Digital (MTD) digital audit trails
  • Domestic Reverse Charge & Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) verification
Weeks 5–6

UK Payroll & Pension Administration

Run compliant UK payroll cycles according to HMRC PAYE guidelines and pension enrolment rules.

  • HMRC PAYE Tax Codes (e.g. 1257L) & National Insurance (NIC Class 1)
  • Pensions Auto-Enrolment & NEST Pension portal integration
  • Real Time Information (RTI) submissions: FPS and EPS drafting
Weeks 7–8

UK GAAP & Year-End Accounts

Draft year-end statutory financial statements for UK micro-entities and small businesses.

  • Micro-Entities Regime (FRS 105) disclosures & layout
  • Small Entities Regime (FRS 102 Section 1A) mapping & journals
  • Companies House and HMRC CT600 Corporation Tax mapping
