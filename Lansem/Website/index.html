Zero-Data-Leakage

UK GDPR & VDI Security Lockdown

Our India team works via secure Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) hosted on AWS London servers. They see screen pixels only — raw client data never leaves the UK.

Active

Clipboard Sharing Blocked

Bi-directional copy-pasting between the secure virtual workspace and local physical workstations is disabled to prevent data leakage.

Active

Local Storage Disabled

Workstation hard drives and local downloads are disabled. Staff cannot save client documents to local machines in India.

Active

Print & USB Redirection Blocked

Physical printing and USB ports are completely locked down at our delivery center, blocking raw document transfers.

Active

Static IP & MFA Lockdown

Dual-factor authentication is mandatory for every login. The AWS environment is accessible only from our office static IP.

🔒 UK Law Accountable: All client engagements are contracted through our UK Limited Company under English Law, backed by full Professional Indemnity Insurance with offshore delivery endorsements.
