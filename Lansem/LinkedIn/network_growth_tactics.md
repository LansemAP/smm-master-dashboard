# Lansem LinkedIn Network Growth Tactics

This document outlines the tactical execution plan to scale your LinkedIn connections with decision-makers (UK accountants, solicitors, and SME owners) organically.

---

## 🔍 1. Search Queries & Lead Filtering

To find the right decision-makers, use these Boolean search strings in the LinkedIn search bar:

### For Accounting Firms:
*   **Search Query**: `Partner AND (Accounting OR Bookkeeping OR Tax)`
*   **Filters**:
    *   *Location*: United Kingdom
    *   *Industry*: Accounting, Financial Services
    *   *Company Size*: 1-10, 11-50 employees (these boutique partners deal with capacity bottlenecks directly and are easy to reach).

### For Law Firms (ALSP Targets):
*   **Search Query**: `("Managing Partner" OR "Director") AND Law`
*   **Filters**:
    *   *Location*: United Kingdom
    *   *Industry*: Law Practice, Legal Services
    *   *Company Size*: 1-10, 11-50 employees.

### For SME Targets:
*   **Search Query**: `("Managing Director" OR "Founder" OR "COO") AND (SaaS OR "E-commerce" OR Agency)`
*   **Filters**:
    *   *Location*: United Kingdom
    *   *Industry*: Software Development, E-learning, Advertising Services
    *   *Company Size*: 11-50, 51-200 employees.

## ⚡ 2. The "10-2-1" Daily Engagement Routine

To grow your account without triggering LinkedIn’s spam/automation flags, follow this manual daily routine:

```
[ Comment on 10 target posts ] ==> [ Send 2 targeted connection requests ] ==> [ Publish 1 thought leadership post ]
```

### Phase 1: Commenting on 10 Target Posts
Do not pitch in comments. Add high-value, technical insights.
*   **The Blueprint**: Find a post by a UK accountant complaining about HMRC portal downtime, capacity limits, or scaling.
*   **Example Comment response**:
    > *"Excellent points on the capacity trap, [Name]. We've seen many firms trying to solve this by hiring local bookkeepers, but the margins just don't support it anymore. White-labeling compliance work overnight has been a game-changer for firms looking to scale client advisory without adding fixed UK overhead. Looking forward to your next post!"*
*   **The Impact**: This places your profile in front of their entire network, driving inbound profile views.

### Phase 2: Send 2 Connection Requests
Send exactly two highly targeted connection requests per day with a personalized message (using templates in the Outreach Playbook). Focus on accounts that have been active on LinkedIn in the last 30 days.

### Phase 3: Publish 1 Thought Leadership Post
Share one post (educational tip, industry update, security blueprint) based on the articles in the Content Strategy library. Consistency trains the algorithm to boost your reach.
